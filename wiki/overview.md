# Overview

Martin Gamsby (senior software dev — C++/Python/Qt/AI, 13+ yrs, 3 patents — and
author, musician, physics-preprint writer; bilingual FR/EN, Quebec) is consolidating
his scattered web presence into **one bilingual personal site** at martingamsby.com,
built with Astro in this repo, hosted on GitHub Pages.

The identity of the site is the multiplicity itself: "I do many things, everything
is connected" (INTP / hyper-fixations framing — his words). Hence [[doors-as-lenses]]:
the homepage offers audience doors (Software, Physics, Book, Music, Everything) that
*filter* a single facet-tagged content pool. [[bilingual-routing]] fixes the biggest
pain of the current setup — two disconnected blog URLs per language.

What exists today: [[jekyll-blogs]] (the content to migrate),
[[github-repos-and-demos]] (the dev-door material), [[profiles-and-channels]]
(the identities to link). The book *Interverti* (sibling repo `Interverti`)
publishes FR-first soon and gets a `/book` landing page — launch timing should
align with it.

Authoritative plan, locked decisions, phases, and open questions: `PLAN.md` at the
repo root (not duplicated here).
