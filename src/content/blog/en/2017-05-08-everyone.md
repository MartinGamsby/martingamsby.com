Whether you're stupid, racist, autistic, controlled by the government, or simply hypocritical, you're probably not aware that your world is different from others'. No matter the signs, the human brain always tries to reassure itself, even if it means staying in an imaginary world all its life. There are probably as many different realities as there are people living on Earth.

Everyone has a problem, a mental illness or a flaw. Really everyone. Without exceptions. Few people know it, and that's maybe for the best. I wonder what my problem is...
