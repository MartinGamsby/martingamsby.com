Be original. Try to avoid phrases like "some spare change to eat," at the risk of being told "don't eat that, you'll choke!" Similarly, avoid arguing on a cellphone.

Passersby tend to avoid giving you their change if it's to pay your monthly plan. They'll feel like they have less money than you.
