Einstein is without a doubt the symbol of intelligence. When we think of a genius, we think of Einstein.

But few know that he couldn't even tolerate physical contact from his own children. He was solitary, addicted to his mathematics, had fits of anger, learned to speak very late, and didn't like crowds. For these reasons, among others, many think he was autistic.
