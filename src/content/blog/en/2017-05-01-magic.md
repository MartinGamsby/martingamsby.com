If magic existed, we would call it science. Magic is just mystery.

If it's demystified (and it always ends up being), it loses its magical status and becomes science, or a visual trick - an illusion - that has been perfected with scientific methodology. In this sense, either magic doesn't exist, or it's everywhere. It's up to you to decide.
