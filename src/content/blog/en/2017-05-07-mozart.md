Mozart was a great musician and composer. He, for example, learned to play a piece of music in only twenty minutes... at 4 years old, without anyone's help, with only a sheet of music.

When he started becoming known and getting music commissions, he could spend days alone, without writing anything, to write a masterpiece in a few hours just before delivering his scores, only transcribing what he had composed entirely in his head. Despite his musical genius, he wasn't the most successful composer, since he was too focused on his music rather than his career.

He ended his life poor, buried in a common grave. For these reasons, among others, many believe he was autistic or had Asperger's.
