"Déjà vus" are actually reconstructions by the brain of a similar situation that has already occurred, since storing all the details would be useless and too costly for our lazy, or rather economical, body. So there's nothing special about it, it's not a premonition, nor a special power, and there's nothing spiritual about it. We could also call déjà vus "brain farts."
