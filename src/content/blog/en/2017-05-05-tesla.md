Tesla, it's said, could see how electrical circuits would work, in his head only. Maybe because he invented practically all the components, something that would probably have taken hundreds of years without him, without his global vision of things that gave a goal to reach.

Without planetary free energy distribution in mind, Tesla probably wouldn't have invented everything he did, and we would probably still be in the early stages of electrical components, still essentially running on coal and oil. Unfortunately, his genius was hidden by his isolation in his art.

Uninterested in money, too passionate about his work, he was exploited by several profiteers. Ironically, due to lack of funding, he couldn't finish his work, while letting others use his patents. For these reasons, among others, many think Tesla was autistic.
