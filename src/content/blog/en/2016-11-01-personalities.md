On the Internet, there are four types of personalities.

1. "Profiteers": make money off others.

2. "Exploited": give money to profiteers, or contribute unwillingly, by watching ads on "free" sites.

3. "Altruists": sell real value, would give if they didn't need money to survive... Some don't even ask for any.

4. "Trolls": seem to take pleasure in annoying people.
